Beef Noodle Soup

Description

Beef Noodle Soup is a perfect orchestration of tender beef, a rich and slightly spicy broth, fresh noodles, a little bok choy, and that absolutely necessary fistful of Chinese pickled mustard greens and fresh scallions and cilantro.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Noodles
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. chop the beef
  2. cook the soup
  3. add the cilantro
