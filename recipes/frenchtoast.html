French Toast

Description

This recipe has a touch of warmth from cinnamon, nutmeg and vanilla. Here’s a pro tip: Make sure you don’t over-soak the bread. Fully submerge it in the batter for only a few seconds and let any excess drip off before cooking. And wait for the bubbles from the melted butter to subside — that’s when you know your pan is hot enough.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • butter
  • syrup

Steps

  1. make a dough
  2. melt the butter
  3. pour the syrup
